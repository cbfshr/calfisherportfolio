Programmable LED Bottle Cap Deer Head Sign

Another project started with my father, but this one took a while to complete. I initially messed up the epoxy pour for the back piece, which was a pretty big set-back. This is made out of two stained pieces of wood: we used a jigsaw to cut out the deer head outline on the front piece, and a CNC machine to router out the back for the bottle caps and epoxy. There is a programmable LED strip lining the inside, which is controlled by a custom circuit with an ATTiny microcontroller chip.
