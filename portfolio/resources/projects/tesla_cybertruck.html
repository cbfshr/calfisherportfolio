Tesla Cybertruck

Source: Tesla Cybertruck by wov

Summer 2020, Personal Project

Many, many hours went into printing this model. After several failed prints, I had given up on it. But I finally decided to take the time to print the remaining parts and put it together. The end result was worth the effort! I printed in PLA, then used silver spray paint to coat the body of the truck and a matte black spray paint for the chassis and rims. The windows and wheels are default black PLA.
