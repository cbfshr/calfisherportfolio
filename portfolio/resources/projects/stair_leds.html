Motion Sensor Stair LEDs

Source: brfshr/stairleds

This project was a cross-collaboration with my cousin who was interested in learning how to program Arduinos and LED strips. His stairs were a perfect candidate for this project and would allow him to navigate his home easier at night.

We used an Arduino UNO, two PID sensors, and an LED strip like the one used in the Programmable LED Mountain Lights project.
