Turtle Tank

I have a white-throated mud turtle named Artemis! He's the chillest dude you'll ever meet. While feeding him or cleaning out his tank, it became tedious to remove his heat lamp and UV light from the tank cover, so I designed and 3D printed two parts that would keep these lights secured.

Automated Turtle Feeder

I also designed and printed a custom automated turtle feeder! I spent several days rapidly prototyping a solid design before going on a week-long vacation. That way I could relax knowing he was being fed. Fun fact: turtles can actually go long periods of time without eating, so if the automation didn't work, it wouldn't be the end of the world! Luckily it has been working great, aside from a few pieces of food getting stuck every now and again.

The turtle feeder has 10 slots in the hopper, a magnetic lid, a 360 degree servo, and an Arduino UNO. I can program it to dispense food at any interval.
